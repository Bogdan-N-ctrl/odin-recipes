Cherry Pie

Description

This cherry pie recipe is the 1999 American Pie Council's National Pie Championship first-place winner in the Fruit and Berry category.

Ingredients

  • all-purpose flour
  • salt, shortening, and cold water
  • pitted sour cherries
  • white sugar
  • cornstarch
  • butter
  • almond extract

Steps

  1. Place cherries, sugar, and cornstarch in a pan. Let sit until the juices begin to release.
  2. Bring the mixture to a boil, then simmer until the juices are thickened.
  3. Remove from the heat, then stir in the butter and almond extract. Let cool.
